Why yimsg Why yimsg

Three pillars built for business 为企业而生的三大核心优势

Data sovereignty, one-minute deploy, and extreme efficiency — all in a machine you own. 把数据主权、极简部署与极致性能，全部装进一台属于你自己的机器。

🔒

Data Sovereignty数据自主可控

All data stays on your own machine in your office. Never touches any third-party cloud. One-click complete purge when needed. 所有数据都掌握在自己手上，集中在办公室的一台机器里，不经过任何第三方云，完全由你掌控。需要时可一键删除，彻底清空。

🚀

One-Minute Deploy极简部署

One machine, up and running in minutes. No complex ops. Works with or without a domain name or public IP — reliable connectivity even in worst-case networks. 一台机器、几分钟即可上线，无需复杂运维。无论是否拥有域名或公网 IP，都有对应接入方式，最坏网络下也能稳定连通。

⚡

Extreme Efficiency极致性能

100+ concurrent users on a 2-core 4GB machine. Deployment under 32MB. Bounded client memory — perfect for resource-constrained embedded scenarios. 2 核 4G 单机即可支撑百人聊天，部署包不到 32MB，客户端内存可控，天然适用于资源受限的嵌入式场景。
