Why yimsg

Three core advantages built for business

Data sovereignty, minimal deployment, and top performance — all packed into a machine that's entirely yours.

🔒

Full data sovereignty

All your data stays in your own hands, centralized on a machine in your office, with no third-party cloud involved, fully under your control. Wipe it clean with one click whenever you need to.

🚀

Minimal deployment

One machine, up and running in minutes, with no complex operations required. Whether or not you have a domain or a public IP, there's a matching connection path — reliable even on the worst networks.

⚡

Top performance

A 2-core, 4GB machine supports hundreds of concurrent chatters, the deployment package is under 32MB, and client memory usage is bounded — naturally suited to resource-constrained embedded scenarios.
